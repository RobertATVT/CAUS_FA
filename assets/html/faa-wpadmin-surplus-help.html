TRANSFER REQUEST WORKFLOW
TICKET INFORMATION
VT PTAG
The VT Property Tag of the item that the Transfer Request was created for.
OWNER PID
The faculty or staff member PID that is the current custodian of the item and is requesting the asset be transferred to Surplus.
DATE
The date that the Surplus Request was initiated by the asset custodian.

Step 2 - The popup window will ask if this asset falls under the IT Department Area of Influence. This is meant for computers and other devices that will need to be handled by the IT Staff to be prepared for the faculty or staff receiving the item. Examples include:

a) Computers that need to be wiped.
b) Tablets that need to be wiped.

If the item falls under this category select "YES".
IF YES - The next portion of the popup will ask that you select the IT person that you wish to assign the ticket to. The app will list all applicable IT personel that are capable of taking the ticket. Once you click "CONTINUE" you will receive a notice that the ticket is transferred to that IT person and it will be removed from your Pending Transfers list and place it under the queue for the selected IT Person.

If the item does NOT fall under the IT Department select "NO"
