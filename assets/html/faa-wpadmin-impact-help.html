TRANSFER REQUESTS
#
The total number of transfer requests that are available to you to be resolved. These items are sent to only the appropriate organization for which they fall under. For example, transfers originating from the Dean's Suite will only be seen by the College Business Manager, the Fixed Assets Coordinator, and the Fixed Assets Liaison in charge of Dean's Suite Items. Trasnfer items are viewable by the following personel:
          College Business Manager
          Fixed Assets Coordinator
          Appropriate School Business Manager
          Appropriate School Fixed Assets Liaison
#
The total number of transfers that are over 14 days old. This number is calculated from the day that the transfer was initiated by the faculty/staff wanting to hand off the piece of equipment.
#
The total number of new transfers within the last 7 days. This number is calculated the same as the 14 day number above.
